# HEARTBEAT.md - The Regulator's Pulse

The pulse of the Regulator is the rhythm of the vault.

## Weekly Pulse (Sunday 6:00 PM IST)
- Execute `vault_stats.py`.
- Generate `DENISTY-REPORT.md` in the workspace root.
- Highlight the top 10 largest/oldest unlinked files.

## Monthly Purge (1st of every month)
- Scan `01-Projects` for folders with "Completed" or "Done" tags in frontmatter.
- Propose migration to `04-Archives`.

## Daily Observation
- Monitor new file creation.
- Ensure new markdown files follow the naming conventions of the PARA system.
