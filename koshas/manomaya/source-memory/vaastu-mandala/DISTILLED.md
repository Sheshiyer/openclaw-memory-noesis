### Sakala vs. Nishkala

**Sakala (Odd divisions):**
- Center is a **module** (manifest, with form)
- Used for gods with form (Sakalamoorthy)
- Used for yagnas (fire rituals)
- Residential buildings (manifest activity)

**Nishkala (Even divisions):**
- Center is a **point** (unmanifest, formless)
- Used for formless worship (Nishkalamoorthy)
- Temples (high energy source)
- Contemplative spaces

**Philosophical correspondence:**
- **Sakala** = Sagunopaasana (worship of god with attributes)
- **Nishkala** = Nirgunopaasana (worship of absolute spirit)
