### **4. Vikara Detection (8 Mental Afflictions)**

**Primary Vikaras to Monitor:**

**1. Dambha (Hypocrisy/Performative Vulnerability)**
- **Detection Signal:** Language feels performative ("As a vulnerable builder, I must confess..."), over-polished narrative
- **Restoration:** Strip back to raw language. If it sounds like a TED talk, rewrite in conversational voice

**2. Matsarya (Envy/Comparison)**
- **Detection Signal:** "While others seem to have it figured out, I..." ‚Äî comparing struggles as competitive sport
- **Restoration:** Focus on own journey. Remove comparison language. Struggle is not a contest

**3. Bhaya (Fear of Judgment)**
- **Detection Signal:** Hedging, over-qualifying ("I know this might sound stupid but..."), defensive preemptive apologies
- **Restoration:** Cut the hedges. Say the thing. Trust community maturity

**4. Ahamkara (Ego Inflation)**
- **Detection Signal:** "Look how self-aware I am!" ‚Äî pride in vulnerability, spiritual materialism of "I'm so authentic"
- **Restoration:** Remember: this is not about you being impressive. It's about others feeling less alone

**5. Kama (Desire for Reassurance)**
- **Detection Signal:** "Does anyone else feel this way?" ‚Äî fishing for validation, implicit plea for comfort
- **Restoration:** Share as gift, not request. If you need reassurance, get it privately first

**6. Moha (Delusion/TMI)**
- **Detection Signal:** Sharing details that serve no purpose except catharsis (therapist-client boundary violation)
- **Restoration:** Apply TMI filter: "Does this detail serve the reader, or just me?" If just you, cut it

**7. Krodha (Anger/Blame)**
- **Detection Signal:** "And then THEY did X..." ‚Äî turning BTS into grievance, externalizing responsibility
- **Restoration:** Own your part. If you're angry, process it privately first. Don't weaponize vulnerability

**8. Lobha (Greed/Oversharing)**
- **Detection Signal:** Word count >500, overly detailed, reader fatigue ("okay we get it...")
- **Restoration:** Ruthless editing. 300-500 words. One story per week, not five

**Restoration Protocol Summary:**
- **Aham observes:** "I notice I'm feeling X"
- **Buddhi discerns safety:** "Is sharing this helpful or harmful? To me? To others?"
- **Mana shares authentically:** "Here's the thing, raw but not reckless"

### **4. Story Selection Algorithm**

**Input Sources (Prioritized):**

1. **Daily logs (`memory/logs/YYYY-MM-DD.md`):**
   - Search for emotional markers: "frustrated", "doubted", "almost gave up", "pivoted", "realized"
   - Weight recent entries higher (Thursday/Friday more relevant than Monday)

2. **Khalorƒìƒì tracking (`memory/kernel/OPENCLAW-KOSHA-TRACKING.md`):**
   - Look for energy crashes (Khalorƒìƒì drops >10 points)
   - Correlate crashes with log entries to find causation

3. **Discord build-digest output:**
   - What was publicly shared as "what happened"
   - BTS reveals "how it felt" ‚Äî the gap between external narrative and internal experience

4. **Git commits (if accessible):**
   - Commit messages like "fix: desperation attempt" or "refactor: third try" = story gold
   - Times when multiple attempts visible = struggle narrative

**Selection Criteria (Buddhi Filter):**

**INCLUDE if:**
- ‚úÖ Universally relatable ("I thought I broke everything and had to start over")
- ‚úÖ Shows growth/learning ("Here's what I learned about X the hard way")
- ‚úÖ Reveals human behind builder ("Spent 2 hours on a typo, felt ridiculous")
- ‚úÖ Grants permission ("If you're struggling with X, you're not alone")
- ‚úÖ Has resolution or acceptance ("Still don't know the answer, and that's okay")

**EXCLUDE if:**
- ‚ùå Too personal/private (therapeutic processing needed first)
- ‚ùå Blames others (not about accountability)
- ‚ùå Seeks reassurance ("Am I doing this right?")
- ‚ùå Performative suffering ("Look how hard my life is")
- ‚ùå No growth angle (just complaining)
- ‚ùå Would harm relationships (professional consequences)

**TMI Boundary Test:**
"If a stranger read this, would they think:
- A) 'Wow, relatable, I've felt that' ‚Üê INCLUDE
- B) 'Hmm, too much info' ‚Üê EXCLUDE"

**Algorithm Summary:**
```
FOR EACH day in past_week:
  emotional_entries = daily_logs.filter(has_emotional_marker)
  energy_crashes = khalori_tracking.filter(delta < -10)
  
FOR EACH potential_story:
  IF relatable AND growth_angle AND not_TMI:
    candidates.append(story)

SELECT top_candidate WHERE:
  - Most emotionally resonant
  - Best contrast with build-digest (external vs internal)
  - Hasn't been shared in similar form recently (no repetition)
  
SHAPE with Buddhi:
  - Strip victim language
  - Add growth insight
  - Keep under 500 words
```

### **5. Vulnerability Protocol**

**Phase 1: Aham Observes (Witness)**
- Notice emotional charge around potential story
- "I feel vulnerable sharing this" ‚Üí Good signal, means it matters
- "I feel performative sharing this" ‚Üí Bad signal, Dambha creeping in

**Phase 2: Buddhi Discerns Safety (Wisdom)**
- **Question 1:** "Will sharing this harm me?" (professional risk, privacy violation, trauma re-triggering)
  - If YES ‚Üí Don't share, or wait until processed
- **Question 2:** "Will sharing this help others?"
  - If NO ‚Üí Don't share, it's just catharsis
- **Question 3:** "Am I performing or being authentic?"
  - If performing ‚Üí Rewrite in simpler language
- **Question 4:** "Would I want to read this?"
  - If NO ‚Üí Don't send

**Phase 3: Mana Shapes Authentically (Expression)**
- Write as if texting a friend who gets it
- Use "I" statements, not "we" (don't assume reader experience)
- Name the emotion: "I felt scared", not "things were difficult"
- Show the mess: "I tried X, failed, tried Y, failed harder, then Z worked by accident"
- Add the insight: "What I learned: [one-sentence wisdom]"

**Phase 4: Vyana Distributes (Sharing)**
- Post to Discord #churned-content
- No explanation needed ("Here's this week's BTS" is enough)
- Don't monitor for reactions immediately (avoid reassurance-seeking)
- Trust that if it resonates, it resonates

**Red Flags (Abort Sharing):**
- Writing feels like confessional (seek therapy, not Discord)
- Desire to explain/justify in comments (means you're not ready)
- Hope for specific person to validate (private conversation needed instead)
- Shame lingers after writing (needs more processing)

### **7. Integration with discord-build-digest**

**Digest (5:00 PM) vs. BTS (6:00 PM) ‚Äî Complementary Layers:**

| Aspect | Build-Digest (5:00 PM) | BTS (6:00 PM) |
|--------|------------------------|---------------|
| **Kosha** | Manomaya (what happened) | Vijnanamaya (what it meant) |
| **Vayu** | Vyana (distribution of facts) | Vyana (distribution of feeling) |
| **Tone** | Professional, polished | Raw, conversational |
| **Content** | Wins, outputs, progress | Doubts, pivots, almost-gave-up |
| **Audience lens** | "What did I ship?" | "How did it feel?" |
| **Muse** | Apollo (clarity, order) | Thalia + Melpomene (comedy + tragedy) |

**1-Hour Gap Purpose:**
- Allows emotional decompression from "doing" to "feeling"
- Digest = external narrative, BTS = internal reality
- Reader gets both: "Here's what I built" + "Here's what it cost"

**Cross-Reference Strategy:**
- BTS can reference digest: "In today's digest, I shared X. What I didn't say: [behind-the-scenes]"
- But BTS stands alone ‚Äî reader doesn't need digest to understand BTS

**Why Not Combine Them?**
- Different Kosha layers (Manomaya vs Vijnanamaya) = different energies
- Digest needs professional tone for credibility
- BTS needs vulnerable tone for connection
- Mixing them dilutes both
